Help Center & FAQ

Everything you need to know about Asta Mart

For Buyers

How do I receive my account after purchasing?
Once you find an account you want, click "Reveal Seller Contact." You will be provided with the seller's Discord or WhatsApp. You coordinate the payment and credential transfer directly with them. Asta Mart acts as the discovery platform, not the payment processor.

What if the account details are wrong?
Since transactions are handled directly between users, we recommend always asking for screen-shares or proof of ownership before sending funds. If a seller provides invalid details, report them immediately to our support team so we can ban their IP and hardware from the marketplace.

Are these accounts full access?
Listings will clearly state whether they include the "Original Email" or if they are "Transfer to New Mail." Always confirm this with the seller before purchasing.

For Sellers

How much does it cost to list an account?
Listing your account on Asta Mart is currently 100% free. You keep 100% of your profits since you deal directly with the buyer.

How long does it take for my listing to be approved?
To ensure marketplace quality, our automated system and manual moderators review listings. This usually takes between 1 to 12 hours. Ensure your Riot Sync was successful to speed up this process.
